Why Cats Can Be So Lazy

A Fun Look at Our Furry Sleep Experts!

Hello, Little Feline Fan!

Have you ever wondered why cats love to snooze all day long? Some cats can sleep for 12 to 16 hours—or even more—every single day! That's almost like having a part-time job in napping.

Cats are natural hunters at night, so they often spend daytime dozing. They also love to lounge around in sunny spots—like little furry solar panels! People sometimes say cats are lazy, but they're really saving their energy for big bursts of playtime (especially when a toy mouse is around).

Not every cat is a sleepyhead—some are quite active! But many cats know how to relax better than any other pet. If they could talk, they'd probably say, "Why stand when you can sit, and why sit when you can nap?"

Did You Know? When cats sleep, they might appear lazy, but they stay partly alert. Their ears can pick up sounds even while snoozing, so they’re ready to jump up if something exciting happens—like a can opener!
